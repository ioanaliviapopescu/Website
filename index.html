Passion brings our hearts and minds togheter

Although photography tends to be an one man kind of job, I found teamwork very helpful and exciting. You may have an idea, even a picture in mind of what you want to capture, what details you want to focus on, but when you have other people, artists or maybe just friends that want to lend a hand, their suggestion can offer you a totally different perspective that ends up giving an unexpectedly amazing result, so different than what you initially came up with.

As Destin Sparks said,

Photography is the story I fail to put into words.

We are here to share our passion and support other artists

I decided to not only offer my personal content, but to provide other artists with the opportunity to put up pictures for sale as well. Therefore, we can all support eachother in these though times we are currently facing during the global pandemic. Therefore, anyone can create an account, upload content, select a price, buy other pictures and so on. More details are provided in the destined sections.
